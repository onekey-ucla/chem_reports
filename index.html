Cross-cutting survey signals

Means use the LMS numeric code −2 (strongly disagree) through +2 (strongly agree); neutral = 0. The same three attitude statements are asked before and again after the video; following charts summarize cohort means (blank responses omitted row-wise).

1. Thinking back to before you watched the video, please select your level of agreement with the following statements:

Strongly disagree · Disagree · Neutral · Agree · Strongly agree

  • I see a connection between chemistry and real-world topics.
  • I am excited to learn organic chemistry.
  • I am nervous about taking organic chemistry.

2. Now that you have watched the video, please select your level of agreement with the following statements:

Strongly disagree · Disagree · Neutral · Agree · Strongly agree
